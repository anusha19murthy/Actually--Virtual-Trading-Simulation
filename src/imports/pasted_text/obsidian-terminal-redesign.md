Redesign the entire UI of a stock market simulation web app called "Actually — Stock Trading Game". The current design uses a purple gradient theme and needs a complete overhaul. The new design should feel like a premium, modern fintech terminal — think Bloomberg meets Linear meets Vercel. Dark and sharp, not colorful or playful. Every element should feel intentional, minimal, and data-dense without feeling cluttered.
OVERALL AESTHETIC DIRECTION — The new identity is "Obsidian Terminal". Near-black backgrounds with cool blue-gray tones. A single electric cyan-green accent color for all positive actions, highlights, and CTAs. Razor-thin borders. Monospaced numbers everywhere. Subtle grid texture in the background. No gradients except for very subtle card depth. No emoji icons — replace all with clean line icons (Lucide or Phosphor style). Typography should feel editorial and technical at the same time.
